# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** February 17, 2026

Metis GRC ("we", "our", "us") operates the metis-grc.com website and the app.metis-grc.com platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

## Data We Collect

- **Account Information:** Name, email address, company name, and role when you register.
- **Usage Data:** Pages visited, features used, and interactions within the platform.
- **Contact Information:** Name, email, company, and message content when you contact us.
- **Analytics Data:** Anonymized browsing data collected via Google Analytics.

## How We Use Your Data

- Providing and improving our services
- Communicating with you about your account and our products
- Analyzing usage patterns to improve the platform
- Complying with legal obligations

## Data Storage and Security

All data is hosted within the European Union. We use encryption at rest and in transit, row-level security, and comprehensive access controls.

## Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation, you have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Rectify inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Restrict processing
- Data portability
- Object to processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at sales@metis-grc.com.

## Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, email us at sales@metis-grc.com.
