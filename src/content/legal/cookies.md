# Cookie Policy

**Last updated:** February 17, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Metis GRC uses cookies and similar technologies on metis-grc.com.

## What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us understand how you use our site and improve your experience.

## Cookies We Use

### Essential Cookies
Required for the website to function properly. These cannot be disabled.
- Session management
- Security features

### Analytics Cookies
Help us understand how visitors interact with our website. We use Google Analytics with anonymized IP addresses.
- Page views and navigation patterns
- Device and browser information
- Geographic region (country-level only)

### Preference Cookies
Remember your settings and preferences, such as language selection.

## Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through our cookie consent banner or through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect your experience on our website.

## Your Rights

Under EU regulations, you have the right to:
- Be informed about cookies we use
- Accept or reject non-essential cookies
- Withdraw consent at any time

For questions about our cookie practices, contact us at sales@metis-grc.com.
